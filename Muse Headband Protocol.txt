Muse headband data collection protocol:

Preparation: 
1. Ensure Muse 2 Headband has at full charge/ battery
2. Wipe the surface electrode pads with a soft cloth to remove debris
3. Clean participants forehead and behind-ear areas to remove excess body oils as well as any makeup
4. Ensure long hair is moved away from electrode contact points 

Placement: 
5. Place headband on forehead approx. 1cm above the eyebrows. 
6. Ensure the rear sensors are snug and tight behind the ears

Signal Verification: 
7. Connect Muse to the 'Muse Connect' application and ensure their is stable connection 

During Recording:
8. Do not make unnecessary movements during the data collection process. Do not adjust headband after it's set in place 

After Use:
9. Remove headband carefully 
10. Wipe down the electrodes using a soft cloth
11. Charge headband fully 
12. Store in the protective case
